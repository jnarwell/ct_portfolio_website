Biography:

James "Jamie" Marwell is an incoming freshman at Stanford, intending to study mechanical engineering. To advance his study in this feild and elevate his electro-mechanical engineering projects he decided to take this course. The goal of his participation is to build a solid foundation in full-stack programming so as to serve as a stepping stone to greater programing skill. Jamie particularly hopes that this course will allow him to delve deeper into the world of Artificial Intellegence. Beyond his love of engineering, Jamie spends hours in used bookstores, reading, playing games, studying history, and working on personal building projects.
